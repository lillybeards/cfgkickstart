lilly frances beards

fibre art / identity / code


lilly is a fibre artist living in manchester and working from islington mill, salford.

a 2023 graduate from manchester school of art, her creative practice explores the intersections of vulnerability, technology, narrative, and fibre.

her practice spans art, design, and craft disciplines, integrating them into creative research for social awareness and initiating open and honest conversation.

she is currently exploring autistic identity, conscious unmasking, and how spaces can be carved for autistic people in the often overwhelming worlds of art and design.
